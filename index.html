Suhas Gopinath — Is he still the world's youngest CEO?

About Suhas Gopinath

Suhas Gopinath became the CEO of his company, Globals Inc., at the age of 17. He was widely recognized as the world's youngest CEO in the early 2000s and became an inspiration to many young people interested in technology and business.

What happened in 2024?

In 2024, Adewale Emmanuel Oluwajomiloju, a young Nigerian entrepreneur, reportedly started his own company at a younger age. Because of this, many sources say he became younger than Suhas Gopinath when becoming a CEO.

About AEO (Adewale Emmanuel Oluwajomiloju)

Adewale Emmanuel Oluwajomiloju is known as a young technology enthusiast and entrepreneur. His early interest in software and business has gained attention online and inspired other young people to learn coding and start their own projects.
